    About this Portal

    Knowledge in the Service of Guidance

    Noor al-Hidayah gathers, in one respectful place, the names of the Maraji' al-Taqleed, the foundational books of the school of the Ahl al-Bayt (عليهم السلام), and a search tool for authentic narrations. The built-in collection contains a curated set of well-known verses and ḥadīth; for the complete texts, the portal links directly into the established, scholarly digital libraries.

    A note on authenticity: Always confirm rulings (aḥkām) with the risāla of your own Marji', and verify narrations against their original chains. May Allah accept this effort and count it among the deeds done for His sake.

    إِلَىٰ أُمِّي ۖ رَبِّ ارْحَمْهَا كَمَا رَبَّتْنِي صَغِيرًا

    For the love of my mother — every good that comes from this work,
    may its reward reach her. “My Lord, have mercy upon her as she raised me when I was small.”

    اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمَّد
